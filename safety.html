Safety and Guidelines

Safety Guidelines

  • Always wear your seatbelt during carpooling trips.
  • Verify the driver and vehicle information before the trip.
  • Share your trip details with someone you trust.
  • Report any concerns or issues to Nexus Pool immediately.

Carpooling Guidelines

Follow these guidelines to ensure a pleasant carpooling experience:

  • Be punctual and arrive at the pickup point on time.
  • Respect the driver's vehicle and personal space.
  • Agree on the payment method and fare before the trip.
  • Adhere to all local traffic and safety regulations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on Nexus Pool?

To create an account, visit our homepage and click the "Sign Up" button. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the registration process.

How can I book a carpooling trip?

Log in to your Nexus Pool account, enter your destination and pickup location, and browse available trips. Select a trip and follow the booking process to reserve your seat.

Is carpooling safe with Nexus Pool?

Absolutely! We prioritize safety and verify all drivers and passengers. You can also share trip details with someone you trust for added security.
